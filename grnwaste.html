Brown bin usage

It is always preferable to use the compost bins rather than the brown bins but for pernicious weeds or woody material use the brown bins. The brown bin can be used for:

  • grass
  • plants
  • leaves
  • hedge clippings
  • branches
  • flowers

It cannot be used for:

  • soil
  • paper
  • glass
  • pet Waste
  • plastics
  • DIY wood
  • stones and rubble
  • cans
  • food
